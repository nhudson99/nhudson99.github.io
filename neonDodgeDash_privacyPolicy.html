Privacy Policy for Neon Dodge Dash

Last updated: November 12, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Neon Dodge Dash ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you play our mobile game. We believe in transparency, so this policy clearly outlines what data we collect and what we don't.

Key Point: We collect minimal data and store it only locally on your device. We do not collect personal information, detailed analytics, or transmit your data to external servers.

2. Information We Store Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted anywhere:

Game Progress

  • High Score: Your best game score
  • Ad Counter: A simple counter to show ads every 3 games

This data stays on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the game.

3. Advertising

Our game displays advertisements to support development. When ads are shown:

  • Ad networks may collect standard advertising identifiers
  • Basic device information may be used for ad targeting
  • We do not control or access this advertising data
  • Ads are shown every 3 completed games

You can learn more about advertising practices by reviewing your device's advertising settings and the privacy policies of advertising networks.

4. Permissions We Request

Our game requests only these device permissions:

  • Internet: Required to display ads and submit high scores to leaderboards
  • Vibration: Provides haptic feedback when you crash

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or files.

5. What We Don't Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Detailed device information beyond what's needed for ads
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Account information
  • Social media information
  • Files or photos from your device

6. Data Security

Since we store minimal data locally on your device:

  • No sensitive data is transmitted over the internet
  • Your game data is protected by your device's security
  • We cannot access your locally stored game data

7. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we collect minimal data that stays on the device, there are no special procedures needed for children's data.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all game data by uninstalling the app
  • Reset your high score by clearing app data in device settings
  • Disable personalized advertising in your device's privacy settings

9. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services:

  • Advertising Networks: For displaying ads (subject to their privacy policies)
  • Google Play Games: For leaderboards (optional, subject to Google's privacy policy)

These services may have their own privacy policies and data practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. International Users

This game is available worldwide. Since we collect minimal data that stays on your device, most international privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) have limited applicability to our data practices.
